# Spotify Speaker Research

This repo contains a small evidence-backed purchasing research pack for compact wireless speakers in the UK.

## Project brief

- [codex_speaker_research_brief.md](codex_speaker_research_brief.md): the original working brief that defined the scope, constraints, and methodology.

## Main report

- [report/final_report.md](report/final_report.md): the buyer-facing final report and shortlist.
- [report/final_audit_notes.md](report/final_audit_notes.md): a short audit summary of what was tightened, softened, or left uncertain in the final pass.

## Structured data

- [data/spotify_verification.json](data/spotify_verification.json): model-by-model Spotify verification, using Spotify's exact device pages as the source of truth for Spotify capability.
- [data/android_risk_log.json](data/android_risk_log.json): evidence log for the Android Google Cast vs Spotify Connect risk assessment.
- [data/candidates.json](data/candidates.json): the final candidate set, shortlist status, recommendation category, pricing notes, and rationale.

## Notes

- [notes/unresolved_questions.md](notes/unresolved_questions.md): unresolved points, caveats, and manual checks still recommended before purchase.
- [notes/research_journal.md](notes/research_journal.md): brief research process log.
